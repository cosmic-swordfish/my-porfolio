I'm a graphic designer based in Nepal with a deep love for character illustration, game aesthetics, and the strange beauty you find in everyday objects — a lavender sprig, a crystal catching light, neon reflections at midnight.

I go by cosmic swordfish on my socials — a name that captures the way I see design: otherworldly but grounded, precise but alive.

Whether you need a distinctive brand identity, expressive illustration, or a UI that actually feels human — I'd love to collaborate. Reach me at hello@rubenshahi.com.np and let's start with a virtual coffee.
